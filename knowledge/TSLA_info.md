# Tesla Inc. (TSLA) - Company Information

## Company Overview
Tesla Inc. is an American electric vehicle and clean energy company founded by Elon Musk and others. The company specializes in electric vehicles, battery energy storage, and solar panel manufacturing.

## Key Products and Services
- **Electric Vehicles**: Model S, Model 3, Model X, Model Y, Cybertruck
- **Energy Storage**: Powerwall, Powerpack, Megapack
- **Solar Products**: Solar panels and Solar Roof
- **Full Self-Driving (FSD)**: Advanced driver assistance software

## Financial Performance (Sample Data)
- **Annual Revenue**: Over $90 billion (2023)
- **Vehicle Deliveries**: Over 1.8 million vehicles annually
- **Gross Margin**: Around 20-25% for automotive
- **Market Cap**: One of the largest automotive companies by market value

## Business Strategy
- Vertical integration from manufacturing to sales
- Focus on battery technology and cost reduction
- Expansion of Gigafactory network globally
- Development of autonomous driving technology
- Energy generation and storage solutions

## Manufacturing Facilities
- **Fremont, California**: Original production facility
- **Shanghai, China**: Gigafactory producing Model 3 and Model Y
- **Berlin, Germany**: European Gigafactory
- **Austin, Texas**: New Gigafactory for Cybertruck and other models

## Technology and Innovation
- Leading battery technology and range
- Over-the-air software updates
- Supercharger network for fast charging
- AI and autonomous driving development
- Manufacturing automation and efficiency

## Competitive Advantages
- First-mover advantage in premium EV market
- Supercharger network infrastructure
- Strong brand and customer loyalty
- Vertical integration and cost control
- Technology leadership in batteries and software

## Risks and Challenges
- Intense competition from traditional automakers
- Production scaling challenges
- Regulatory and safety concerns with autonomous driving
- Dependence on key personnel (Elon Musk)
- Supply chain constraints for batteries and chips
- Quality control and customer service issues

## Growth Opportunities
- Expansion in global markets, especially China and Europe
- New vehicle models (Cybertruck, Semi, Roadster)
- Energy storage business growth
- Full Self-Driving software monetization
- Potential robotaxi service
